Project Overview

Jacob's Vocaloid Showcase

The primary purpose of this website application is to showcase and promote the artistic work of Jacob San Miguel, an up-and-coming artist specializing in vocaloid. The site aims to engage fans, attract new audiences, and provide a platform for professional visibility.

The intended users of the website are primarily fans of vocaloid and music, looking to explore and enjoy his creative works. Industry professionals, such as music producers, event organizers, and fellow artists, will also find value in the site for potential collaborations and insights into his artistic journey. Additionally, the website targets a broader audience of general music enthusiasts and media representatives seeking emerging talent in the vocal arts scene.

The website serves as an engaging platform for showcasing Jacob San Miguel's vocal artistry, offering a visually appealing and informative experience. It highlights his creative journey and style through detailed descriptions and links to his works on external platforms like YouTube and SoundCloud. Designed to attract fans and industry professionals alike, the site emphasizes his achievements and talents, while providing avenues for audience interaction and professional networking.

Site Map

Page Design

HOME

HOME serves as the primary landing page for enthusiasts visiting the website. Its content includes a featured section that will house the latest updates and releases of the artist. This page will contain hyperlinks to songs, with lightboxes around the videos.

Journey

Journey is a page where people interested in learning about the hurdles Jacob has faced to get where he is now, and why he is passionate about Vocaloid, can explore.

Works

Works will be where the main content Jacob is trying to promote will be housed for fans to explore through dropdowns of SoundCloud and YouTube sections with descriptions and links to songs.

Contact

Contact is for anyone who would want to get in touch with Jacob for potential business deals. A data field for an email will be provided, along with a description and validation.

Fan Interaction

Fan Interaction is where a fan can go to click either a random song button or a random trivia button that will be made using arrays. This is a page where fans can expect the unexpected. The random buttons will give the user a link to a song or an interesting question and answer about Jacob.

Dynamic Functionality on the website

We will implement dynamic JavaScript functionalities. On the "Fan Interaction" page, JavaScript will be used for an interactive experience where fans can click to receive random songs or trivia about Jacob, similar to interactive elements seen with google.com. This randomization, created using arrays, ensures a varied experience each time. Additionally, the "Contact" page will feature an interactive form with real-time validation for business inquiries, drawing inspiration from efficient form functionalities with puma.com. These JavaScript features are carefully chosen to make the website more interactive, improve communication, and provide a personalized experience for users.
